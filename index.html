📐 Methodology

"机构不预测事件，机构管理脆弱性。"
— Institutions don't predict events. They manage fragility.

Fragility Score (0-100): Composite of all 16 indicators. Red signals contribute 100%, yellow 40%, green 0%. Score ≥75 = high fragility. ≥12 red signals triggers extreme warning.

Percentile Rank: Each indicator's current value ranked against its 252-day (1-year) history. 90th percentile = higher than 90% of readings in the past year.

Acceleration: The rate of change of the rate of change. Measures whether momentum is building or fading — the institutional edge over retail who only look at direction.

Signal Logic: Thresholds combine absolute levels (e.g., VIX >25) with percentile positioning. No single indicator determines action — fragility emerges when multiple signals align.

Data Sources: Yahoo Finance (VIX, ETFs, indices), calculated proxies for credit spreads, sentiment, and leverage. Updated weekdays at 7:00 AM and 12:30 PM PT.
